About Tabontabon

The municipality is divided into 16 barangays, each overseen by elected officials responsible for local governance. In 2023, Tabontabon ranked 196th in government efficiency among 5th to 6th class municipalities in the Cities and Municipalities Competitive Index, reflecting its commitment to effective public service.

Public Safety

The **Bureau of Fire Protection (BFP)** is the primary government agency in the Philippines responsible for fire prevention, suppression, and investigation. Tasked with ensuring public safety, the BFP enforces fire safety laws, conducts inspections of buildings and establishments, and provides firefighting and emergency response services. It also plays a vital role in disaster risk reduction by educating communities on fire prevention measures and conducting rescue operations during emergencies. Through continuous training, modernization of equipment, and collaboration with other agencies, the BFP remains committed to protecting lives, properties, and the environment from fire-related hazards and disasters.
